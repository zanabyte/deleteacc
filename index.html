Delete Your Flow Account

To delete your Flow account and all associated data:

  1. Open the Flow app.
  2. Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account.
  3. Confirm deletion using your Google account.
  4. All your data (transactions, preferences) will be deleted permanently within 7 days.

If you cannot access the app, email us at zanabytelimited@gmail.com and we will delete your account manually.
